Frequently Asked Questions

What's the bag limit for Economy class for both Carry-on and Check-In?
The Carry-on bag limit should be 7kg for Economy Class passengers and 23kg for Checked baggage. Each passenger is allowed 23kg x 2 and 7kg x 1.
What happens if the airline loses my luggage?
If you're at the airport, please speak to a member of the FlyAfrica team for help. If you've left the airport, please complete our Lost Property Form Lost Property Form to report missing personal items or cabin baggage and we'll respond promptly
What should I do if my baggage is delayed?
If you can't find your baggage at the terminal, you should visit the baggage services desk at the airport. This is usually found in the customs hall near the baggage claim area. When we receive your baggage, we'll contact you to arrange a delivery time, subject to approval by customs.
